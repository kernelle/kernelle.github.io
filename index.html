About This Website

A curious dev on the net,
Pondered life on this planet.
For a web that's decentralized,
Diversified, Open-Source, and KISS-optimized,
One more node on the net was his bet.

No cookies, no tracking, minimalistic design and hosted using federated social media. The Fediverse is a network of different types of social media with the ability to interact with or share content between them. They are typically made to be self-hosted and decentralized, so that each social media type is itself already a web of servers. The content on this blog is sourced from Lemmy, a forum of the Fediverse, where the posts are shared to encourage discussion, feedback, and discoverability.
